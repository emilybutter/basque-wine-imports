Welcome to Basque Wine Imports

Where passion for exceptional wines meets the heartland of America. Founded in 2024 and based in Kansas City, we specialize in curating a unique selection of wines from the Basque region, bringing the bold flavors and storied traditions of this extraordinary area directly to your table.

Our mission is to connect wine lovers with the authentic taste of Basque culture by partnering with artisanal producers who craft wines that embody the spirit of their land. Whether you're a seasoned sommelier or just beginning your wine journey, we're here to share the diversity and excellence of Basque winemaking, one bottle at a time. Cheers to new connections and unforgettable flavors!

Producer Spotlight · 01

A morning at Gorka Izagirre

In the hills above Bilbao, just fifteen minutes from the city, the Izagirre family farms more than forty hectares of steep, southeast-facing vineyards — each one rooted in old-vine Hondarrabi Zuri, Hondarrabi Zerratia, and Hondarrabi Beltza. Cool Atlantic air, constant drizzle, and a sea breeze off the Bay of Biscay shape every bottle.

Quietly opinionated, generously poured, and unmistakably of the place. We brought back four bottles.
