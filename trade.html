What we offer

A focused book — not a catalog

We work with two estates on the Bay of Biscay and bring in what they bottle — sparkling, white, rosé, and a singular red from indigenous Hondarrabi grapes. Tight allocations, transparent pricing, hand-sold support.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you have minimums?

No case minimums in our active markets. We're happy to start a new account with a mixed case while you taste the book.

Can we get samples?

Yes — restaurants and retailers in active markets can request sample bottles. Out-of-market accounts can join our waitlist for shipped samples when we expand.

Do you offer staff trainings?

Yes. In-market accounts can book a 45-minute training for FOH or BOH teams covering Basque wine regions, the three indigenous grapes, and the producers we represent.

Are the wines available by the glass?

Several are well-suited to BTG programs — particularly the Zudugarai White and the Gorka Izagirre. We'll work with you on pricing and program fit.

Where can I find current pricing?

FOB and wholesale pricing is available on request from the trade desk. We don't publish prices publicly to keep pace with allocation changes.
